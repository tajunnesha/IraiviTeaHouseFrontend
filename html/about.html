Where Every Sip is a Melody of Calmness.

At Iraivi, we believe that tea is more than just a beverage; it’s an invitation to pause. Born from a vision of pure tranquility,
Iraivi Tea House was created to be a sanctuary for those seeking a moment of calm in a busy world.

Our name reflects a divine grace, and our space is designed to wrap you in a warm embrace of soothing melodies and earthy.
The heart of our house is the Kulhad Chai—served in traditional clay pots to bring you closer to the earth with every sip.

Our Vision

To brew more than just tea; to create a sanctuary where every sip tells a story of tradition, purity, and togetherness. We envision Iraivi Tea House as the heart of your daily rituals, blending the authentic taste of the past with the vibrant energy of the future.

Our Mission

To serve the perfect cup of tea that rejuvenates the body and soul.
We are committed to sourcing the finest ingredients, maintaining the highest standards of hygiene,
and providing a warm, welcoming space for our community.
At Iraivi, our mission is to turn every tea break into a memorable experience of comfort and quality.

Freshly brewed happiness, everyday!
